# Generation Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing any tailored resume or cover letter.

## Truthfulness

- [ ] Every claim is supported by approved input files.
- [ ] No facts, dates, employers, skills, projects, certifications, or metrics have been invented.
- [ ] Any uncertain claim has been removed rather than guessed.

## JD Relevance

- [ ] The most relevant supported experience has been selected for the specific job description.
- [ ] The resume and cover letter reflect the role's priorities without copying the JD mechanically.
- [ ] Tailoring is limited to emphasis, bullet selection, ordering, and wording.

## ATS-Friendliness

- [ ] Relevant keywords from the job description appear naturally where they are truthfully supported.
- [ ] Wording is clear, concrete, and easy to parse.
- [ ] Formatting remains simple and ATS-friendly.

## Formatting Check

- [ ] Resume body text is Times New Roman 9 pt.
- [ ] Resume section headings are Times New Roman 10 pt.
- [ ] Resume subheadings are Times New Roman 9.5 pt.
- [ ] Cover letter body text is Times New Roman 12 pt.
- [ ] No mixed font families appear in the final documents.
- [ ] No unintended font size drift appears in the final documents.

## Final Layout QA

- [ ] Header and contact block is intact.
- [ ] Education is present.
- [ ] Certifications are present.
- [ ] Bullet formatting is intact.
- [ ] Resume is exactly one page.
- [ ] Cover letter layout is clean and aligned.
